Joint Pain

Ankle Sprain

Any type of pain in physical activities due to ligament injury and muscle rupture, sprain & strain... mainly seen in sports person

Frozen Shoulder

Frozen shoulder is a very painful condition patient unable to do shoulder movements because of severity in pain and stiffness in shoulder joint

Knee Pain

Knee pain is condition due to degenerative changes in knee joint mostly oesto arthritis is seen in this condition joint space reduce, oestophyte formation present, stiffness in knee joint etc.

Ligament Injuries

Ligament injury is a soft tissue injury mainly knee and ankle ligaments mostly seen ...ACL ligament injury is most common condition

Cerebral Palsy

Cerebral palsy is a condition in children by birth , any time of physical deformity due to neurological conditions.

Sciatica

Sciatica is compression in the sciatica nerve because of lumbar radiculopathy.. pain in lower back then radiat to buttock region and posterior aspect of thigh.

Paralysis

Paralysis is a neurological conditions due to injury in brain and internal bleeding.in this condition patient unable to do movements of effected part of the body
